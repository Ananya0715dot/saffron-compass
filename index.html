About Us

Saffron Compass, founded by Ananya Krishnan from Bangalore, is more than a brand — it's a movement to rediscover and celebrate the soul of Indian cuisine and culture. We specialize in creating luxurious, handcrafted experiences that bring people closer to the traditions, stories, and tastes of India's rich heritage.

Our Mission

At Saffron Compass, our mission is to bridge the gap between heritage and modern living. We strive to offer experiences that are not only enjoyable but deeply meaningful — allowing our guests to discover the essence of India through its food, rituals, and regional beauty. Whether it's through storytelling dinners or immersive travel trails, every moment is crafted with intention and authenticity.

What We Offer

  • Curated Culinary Events: Home-style tasting dinners and regional feasts in luxurious settings.
  • Cultural Experiences: Guided heritage walks, local market explorations, and regional cooking sessions.
  • Immersive Travel: Custom-designed travel routes exploring food, culture, and tradition in each destination.
  • Creative Workshops: Learn Indian cooking, food plating, spice pairing, and sustainable dining practices.
  • Pop-Up Events: Unique dining setups in unexplored or iconic locations across India and abroad.

F&B Chain Consulting

We also operate as a strategic consultant for national and international food and beverage brands. From brand identity design to operational efficiency, we help businesses craft memorable customer experiences. Our services include:

  • Brand Positioning & Storytelling
  • Menu Engineering & Culinary Strategy
  • Interior & Atmosphere Concept Planning
  • Marketing, Promotion, and Digital Branding
  • Expansion Planning & Franchise Models

Meet the Founder

Ananya Krishnan is a passionate cultural curator, entrepreneur, and storyteller. Raised in Bangalore and inspired by the diversity of Indian traditions, Ananya created Saffron Compass to bring meaningful, luxurious, and educational experiences to people around the globe. Her work is a fusion of food, heritage, community, and innovation — aimed at building long-lasting impressions and stronger cultural ties.

With a background in hospitality and a deep-rooted love for Indian heritage, Ananya is constantly exploring new regions, flavors, and formats to connect modern audiences with timeless traditions. She believes that every flavor has a story, and every story deserves a platform.
