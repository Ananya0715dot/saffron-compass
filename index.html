About the Brand

Saffron Compass is founded by Ananya Krishnan from Bangalore. We create luxurious, handcrafted cultural and culinary experiences for curious travelers, food lovers, and heritage explorers. Whether it's an intimate home-style tasting dinner, a regional heritage trail, or a creative culinary workshop, our goal is to connect people with stories and flavors that matter.
